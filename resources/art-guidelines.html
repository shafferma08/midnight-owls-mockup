Art Guidelines

How to prep your files for perfect prints.

Vector vs. Raster

Vector (.AI, .EPS, .PDF) is preferred. Vector art can be scaled infinitely without losing quality. We recommend Adobe Illustrator.

Raster (.PSD, .JPG, .PNG) must be at least 300 DPI at actual print size. If your artwork is pixelated, the print will be too.
