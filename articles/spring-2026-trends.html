2. The Technique: Puff Ink

Flat print is fine, but Texture is king right now. "Puff Ink" (a plastisol additive that expands when heated) is being used for retro-inspired typography.

Design Tip: Don't clear-coat the puff. Leave it matte and slightly rough for that authentic 80s/90s sportswear feel. Use it for the main headline, but keep small details flat.
